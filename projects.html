Projects

Major Project - Human Following Robot

We have made a prototype of a human following robot using Arduino Uno and different sensors for detecting distance and following a human within a specific range without any contact. It will be embedded in a smart stretcher and smart trolley.

We have made this project by keeping two problems in mind :
1. In covid- 19 situation the diseases was spreading through physical contact. This smart stretcher will provide contactless movement between patient and nurse.
2. Loading and unloading of heavy objects requires high physical strength which is giving rise to back pain, knee pain may be spinal cord injury.
So in todays market smart stretcher can reduce the physical work of the individual.
